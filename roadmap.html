The Growth Levers

Where the Opportunity Is

Five areas where your practice is leaving patients - and revenue - on the table. None of these require you to work more days or see more clients than you want to.

01

Be Found When They Search

Right now, a woman in Eltham or the wider north-east Melbourne corridor searching "cosmetic nurse near me" may not find you. Your Squarespace site at musebymonique.com.au is live, but without targeted search engine optimisation it's invisible to anyone who doesn't already know your name. The women you want - thoughtful, research-driven, in their 40s - start with Google. They need to find MUSE by Monique there first.

SEO + Visibility
02

Let Monique Shine

You are the product. In a space where trust is everything, women choose their injector based on how they feel about the person behind the needle - not the before-and-after (which you can't show anyway). Educational video content on @muse_bymonique that positions you as a knowledgeable, empathetic expert is your most powerful growth channel. Three short Reels per week on topics like ageing gracefully, what to expect from a consultation, and how to spot a good injector builds trust at scale - while you're seeing patients.

Content + Trust
03

Automate the Admin

You're spending 5+ hours per week on social media alone - plus booking, follow-ups, and client communications - all manually, all yourself. Timely has features you're not using: automated appointment reminders, rebooking prompts, post-appointment follow-ups. Getting that infrastructure turned on is low-cost, fast to set up, and immediately reclaims hours every week.

Quick Win
04

The Rejuran Advantage

Most treatments you offer sit under AHPRA advertising restrictions - which means you can't name them in ads or promotional content. Rejuran is one of the few treatments that doesn't carry the same restrictions. Skin rejuvenation, under-eye quality, and skin health - these are outcomes you can speak to directly. This is a legitimate advertising entry point for cold audiences that doesn't require you to bend any rules.

Advertising Edge
05

Virtual Consult as Your Front Door

You already offer a free 15-minute virtual consultation - that's your front-end offer and you don't need to build one from scratch. The opportunity is using it intentionally: as the call to action on your website, the destination for your content, and the entry point for cold audiences who aren't ready to walk into a clinic but are ready to have a conversation. The consult is where the relationship starts. It's also where most of your competitors have nothing.

Quick Wins You Can Move On This Week

These don't require a full system build. They're fast, low-cost, and will make an immediate difference.

Win 01

Timely Email Reminders

Turn on automated appointment reminders and post-appointment follow-up messages inside Timely. It takes under an hour to set up and immediately reduces no-shows and lapsed clients.

Win 02

Three Educational Reels Per Week

Short videos where you speak directly to camera about topics your patients ask about. Ageing gracefully. What happens in a consultation. How to choose an injector. No treatments named. No before-and-afters. Just you, being the expert you already are.

Win 03

Instagram Bio Optimisation

Your @muse_bymonique bio should immediately tell a new visitor who you are, what you do, where you're based (Eltham VIC), and what to do next - with a direct link to your free virtual consult. Most injector bios are a missed opportunity. Yours doesn't need to be.

Win 04

Website SEO Basics

Page title, meta description, your location, and the words women actually type when searching. These basic fixes take a couple of hours but are the difference between being findable and being invisible to people who don't already know your name.

The Path Forward

Setup Timeline

A phased approach that builds your digital foundation properly, so every piece supports the next.

Phase 1 - Weeks 1-2

Foundation

The infrastructure that everything else builds on.

  • Timely automation - reminders, follow-ups, rebooking prompts
  • Instagram bio and link-in-bio update with virtual consult CTA
  • Website SEO audit and quick-fix implementation
  • Virtual consult landing page live (like the one in this demo)
Phase 2 - Weeks 3-6

Content Engine

Establishing your educational content rhythm so Monique builds visibility on autopilot.

  • Brand voice and content framework defined (so ChatGPT/Canva work faster)
  • 3-Reel per week content strategy - topics, structure, filming tips
  • Rejuran content - skin quality positioning that can run as paid
  • Saved Instagram reply templates for common DMs
Phase 3 - Weeks 6-10

Lead Nurture

Converting warm interest into booked consultations without manual follow-up.

  • Virtual consult intake sequence - what happens before and after
  • Email or SMS follow-up for people who enquire but don't book
  • Seasonal content planning around key decision moments
  • Review of capacity and booking patterns - are Tuesdays and Thursdays optimised?
