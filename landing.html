Real Questions

What Women Ask Before Booking

Every concern here is one I've heard across the consultation table. None of them are silly. All of them deserve a proper answer.

"I've never done anything before. I'm scared of looking overdone."

This is the most common thing I hear - and it's exactly why the consultation matters so much. We talk about what you want and, just as importantly, what you don't want. Understated, natural results are always on the table. Nothing happens without your full understanding first.

"I just want to look like myself - refreshed, not different."

That's my favourite kind of outcome. The goal is never to change your face - it's to help it look as good as it feels when you're at your best. We talk through your specific features, your concerns, and what refreshed actually means to you. Then we work toward that.

"How much time will I need? I'm always busy."

The virtual consult is 15 minutes and you can do it from home or your car between meetings. In-person appointments are typically 45-60 minutes including the full consultation and any treatment. I run to time - I respect that yours matters.

"Is it safe? I've read some scary things online."

I'm a registered nurse with AHPRA oversight, which means the same professional and safety standards that apply to any registered nurse apply to my work here. I won't proceed with anything I'm not confident is right for you, and I'll always explain the risks and aftercare clearly.

"I don't know what I need - I just know something feels off."

You don't need to arrive with a treatment plan. That's what the consultation is for. Tell me what you see in the mirror, what bothers you when you look at photos, or just that you feel tired and you're not sure why. We work it out together - honestly.

"What does it cost? I don't want any surprises."

The virtual consult is completely free. The in-person consultation is $50, which is fully redeemable against any treatment on the day. At the end of our in-person consultation you'll have a clear, personalised plan with full pricing before anything goes ahead. No surprises.

Common Questions

About the Consultation

What happens in the free virtual consultation?

It's a 15-minute conversation - phone or video, whichever you prefer. We talk about what you're thinking about, what your concerns are, and whether an in-person consultation makes sense. There's no pressure to proceed and no obligation to book anything afterwards. It's genuinely just a chat.

How is the $50 consultation deposit used?

The $50 is applied in full to any treatment you choose on the day of your in-person consultation. If you decide not to proceed with treatment after the consultation, the $50 covers the consultation itself. Either way, it's not a hidden fee - it's a way of ensuring the time is used well for both of us.

What if I don't know what I want?

That's completely fine - it's actually quite common. Come with a general sense of what's been bothering you (feeling tired, a feature you've noticed changing, a photo that surprised you) and we'll work through the specifics together. You don't need to arrive with a treatment plan in mind.

Can I come in for a consultation and decide not to proceed?

Absolutely. You'll receive a full recommendation and pricing at the end of the in-person consultation, and you're under no obligation to proceed on the day. Some clients like to think about it, talk to a partner, or simply wait until they feel ready. That's completely respected.

Where is the clinic located?

Monique works from SK.IN Eltham, Shop 8, Midway Arcade, 972/974 Main Rd, Eltham VIC 3095. The clinic is shared with dermal therapists - each running their own independent practice. Full details are confirmed when your appointment is booked.

What days are appointments available?

In-person appointments are available Tuesdays 9am - 5pm and Thursdays 10am - 8pm, plus selected Saturdays (usually the last Saturday of each month with occasional extras). Virtual consultations are more flexible - check current availability when booking.
